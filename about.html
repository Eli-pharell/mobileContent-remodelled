OUR COMPANY

Mobile Content.Com Ltd. (MC.C) is the leading Ghanaian value-added-service provider, the preferred link for music, sports, news, games, health, inspiration etc. on mobile telephony. Our services build a crucial link to entertainment and information for mobile users and MC.C has continuous pioneered lifestyle changing mobile-based products in Ghana and selected West African markets.

MISSION STATEMENT

MC.C connects mobile users with affordable and innovative access to reliable, exciting and up-to-the-minute information though their handsets, utilizing SMS, MMS, IVR and WAP to deliver products with real value to local subscribers.

OUR VISION

Recognizing the unique need of our subscribers and limitations to other ICT structures, MCC provides simple and affordable mobile based services specially designed based on the needs of our markets, products that increase their access to news/information, provide entertainment, expand their knowledge, improve their education and impact on their health and wealth. The ultimate goal is an environment where mobile telephony provides the subscriber with modern means to make informed decisions minimal effort through their handsets!
