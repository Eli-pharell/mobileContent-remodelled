MyCloud

The MyCloud service from Mobile Content.Com is an SMS based application that enables mobile subscribers across Ghana receive short regular messages on relevant topics in sports, health, pregnancy, finance,and movies simultaneously. You just send the preferred keyword to the short code and you are automatically added to the subscriber pool and each time you receive a message, it is in real time and at the most affordable moderate tariff.
